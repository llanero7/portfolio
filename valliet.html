About the app

VALLIET is an application created to complement the Valet Parking service and, at the same time, promote offers in shopping centers and restaurants. When we arrive at a shopping mall and can't find parking, the next step is to request the help of a Valet Parking. The application's purpose is to create a link of responsibility and transparency between the vehicle owner and the parking attendant.

Problem statement

IMNOVATY is a software development company that needed to redesign one of its digital products. The new proposal had to be easy to use, be related to vehicle parking, and connect the car owners with valet parking attendants transparently and securely. See original screens shared by the client.

Logo

For the logo design, I took advantage of the first character, "V", to create an Isotype. I understood that the user could connect with this symbol easily.

Color Palette

These colors are associated with the earth (represent warmth, comfort, and nature). Brown serves as a variant of black, and yellow symbolizes energy. The goal of the selected colors is to convey security, confidence, and sophistication.

Typeface: Montserratte

The old posters and signs in the traditional Montserrat neighborhood of Buenos Aires inspired Julieta Ulanovsky to design this typeface. It rescues the beauty of urban typography that emerged in the first half of the twentieth century.
