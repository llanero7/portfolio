A bit about me

I am a UX/UI product designer with several years of experience. I specialize in user-centered approach, strategic mindset, user experience, and interaction design. I enjoy collaborating with other designers, developers, and salespeople to solve complex challenges.

My passion is to create beautiful and meaningful products that help make people's lives easier.

For the last 17 years, I have been designing and delivering digital experiences for banks, insurance companies, pharmacy chains, online stores.

Since I was a child, I have always wondered how a single device could be entertaining and addictive at the same time, even be used by people regardless of age. That was my Nintendo, the magical box!

As I grew up, I discovered this magic was possible thanks to the intervention of user experience specialists, hundreds of tests, and constant adjustments to keep games and consoles up to date with the changing times.

I have never had the opportunity to create a video game, but I try to make the users feel hooked and attracted to each project I design.
